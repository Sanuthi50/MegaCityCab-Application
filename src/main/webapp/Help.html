Help and FAQ

How do I book a ride?

To book a ride, simply go to the "Book a Ride" section, fill in your details, and click "Book Now". Ensure you are logged in to make a booking.

How do I manage my bookings?

To manage your bookings, go to the "Manage Bookings" section where you can view and update your current and past bookings.

What do I do if I encounter an issue?

If you face any problems, you can contact our support team through the "Contact Us" section. We are available 24/7 to assist you.

How do I logout?

To logout, click the "Logout" button in the navigation bar. This will end your session and you will be logged out of your account.
